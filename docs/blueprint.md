# **App Name**: NeighborBuy

## Core Features:

- Community Bulletin Board: Display local deals, products, and group buy opportunities shared by neighbors, focusing on visual clarity with images and clear pricing information.
- Product Verification: Allow users to post and view real photos and videos of products shared by community members, including price comparisons to identify best deals.
- Neighborhood Trust Scores: Display seller's reliability scores calculated from neighbors' purchase ratings and feedback to facilitate a safe community environment.
- Proximity-Based Sharing: Categorize offers by geographic location (e.g., street or complex) to prioritize local sharing and pick-up opportunities. Filter options according to most immediate needs first.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Vibrant coral (#FF7F50), evokes warmth and community.
- Background color: Soft peach (#FAF0E4), provides a welcoming and gentle backdrop.
- Accent color: Muted teal (#66CDAA), adds a modern, reliable contrast that signifies the ease of the platform.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif, combining a modern and approachable feel.
- Use simple, friendly icons to represent categories (food, household, etc.).
- A clean, card-based layout presenting offers with relevant details and immediate contact options.
- Smooth transitions and feedback animations on user interactions.
